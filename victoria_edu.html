Software Engineer, Victoria Goodnews Augustine Education Journey

Once upon a time, when I was just a young child in primary school, I didn’t take education seriously. I enjoyed playing all day and didn’t know much about learning. School didn’t mean much to me—especially in the early classes. I often felt sad and discouraged whenever my mom asked me to get ready for school.

Everything changed when I got to Primary 5. That year, we had a class teacher who was very committed and serious about our success. Unlike other teachers who were less involved, he motivated us and pushed us to do our best. Because of him, I began to love school. He made learning interesting and gave me the encouragement I needed.

When exam time came, he stood by me and made sure I understood what I was taught. Through his help and God's grace, I passed my exams and came first in the class. I was even given a double promotion straight to Junior Secondary School (JSS1). That was one of the happiest moments of my life.

My secondary school years were amazing. I continued to work hard and stayed at the top of my class. I always came first in every term. I developed a deep love for education and never wanted to miss a day of school. Learning became my passion and I knew that education was the key to a better future.

Now, I am a student at Brigham Young University, pursuing my dreams without stress. I am proud to say that I am becoming a skilled software developer. My journey has not always been easy, but every step has been worth it. I am grateful to God and to all the people who believed in me along the way.

I believe education can transform anyone's life—just like it transformed mine. My story is a testimony that with the right guidance, hard work, and faith, nothing is impossible.

Thank you for reading my story!
